About Me

Hello! I'm Jhon Paul Malubag, a fresh graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology from Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Pasig. My passion for technology drives me to constantly grow and take on new challenges in the field of software and IT development.

I am proficient in a range of programming languages and tools including Java, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, VB.NET, and Bootstrap. I also have solid experience in database management using MySQL. During my 3-month internship at iStaff Solutions Inc., I honed practical IT skills such as troubleshooting, system setup, CCTV/Wi-Fi installation, and computer maintenance, which helped me develop both technical expertise and real-world problem-solving abilities.

I am a fast learner, adaptable, and passionate about programming and continuous improvement. I thrive in team environments and am always eager to explore new technologies and projects that allow me to contribute meaningfully.

Beyond the tech world, I enjoy playing mobile games, watching anime, and staying active through volleyball. These hobbies help me maintain a healthy balance between work and life, and they continuously inspire creativity and teamwork in everything I do.
